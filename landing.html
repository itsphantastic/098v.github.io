About

Let's get Lost

We've come a long way haven't we? However the journey is just beginning.

Total Natural Physique, is a term used when talking about Bodybuilding and achieving with hard work and determination. Of course, there are easier methods people can use to achieve the best physique, but you see those methods aren't about the journey itself. When going on this journey oftentimes its not necessary about the destination itself. A seemingly disastrous wrong turn away from the mainstream work out could lead you to the total natural physique everyone seems to love.

Its a lot like design. Sometimes you start with a goal in mind but get lost along the way. Sometimes you make happy mistakes and end up at places you never would have realized you wanted to go. Design is as much about getting where you need to be as it is about getting started and letting the project morph and evolve with what each step of the process reveals to you about what you started off wanting and what you really needed. That's the beauty of it.
