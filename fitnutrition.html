Fit Nutrition is an augmented reality application that
helps capture the users meals and tells them the nutritional values.

ABOUT
Fit Nutrition uses a unique AI system whereby with each photo captured. The AI records the information and feeds the user, helpful tips and tricks to keep on track to their goal!

Fit Nutrition sources the correct information from Doctors and Nutritionists. Users can be sure that the articles and insights are reliable. Here at Fit Nutrition we want our users to focus on feeling good in their skin, not what social media wants them to look like.

WHY?
Starting and Stick is the main problem when it comes dieting. Fit Nutrition helps you start and stick to a macro induced diet, furthermore it helps find the correct macro nutrition fit for each individuals body because we know everyone is unique and no one person is the same.

My Role

I was the sole creater and visionary on this project. Fit Nutrition was broken up into three distinct projects; an app, website and editioral. Fit Nutrition was created to help improve the food diary experience. From choosing the typeface to designing the UI, was my creation. I did seek out help from my peers and the target market for the application.

Fit Nutrition was my final thesis project.

Challenges

Gathering information for all other students, across nine different programs.

Creating an experience that represents all nine programs equally.

Delivering a fully working website in a short time frame.

Mission Statement

Let's Get Fit Together!

Solution

The solution is to make an all inclusive design that is both informative and appealing to the target audience; which are millennials.

Design Deliverables
There are three different deliverables from a; website, to a moblie application, and lastly an editorial.

The food dairy mobile applicatoin is ruled by one mobile app; My Fitness Pal. Fit Nutrition wants to change the industry and learn more about all its users goals and make sure every goal can be reached. Fit Nutrition uses an unique Augmented reality capturing system, for users to just capture their meals and go.

Of course, with a fast pace on-the-go demographic, the most logical way to deliver the information would be an on-the go mobile application.

Lessons Learned

Having a fresh set of eyes can be a good when to see what you may have missed. When working on such a large project like a, thesis you can feel at times that you as the designer know whats right for the target market. Knowing your audience really helps keep the design direction and choices on the correct path. Constantly asking my peers, for their point of view, seeing the perspective of the end-users. I tried to articulate the clearest path for the deliverables.
