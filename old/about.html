Who am I?







Hey my name is Omar and I'm just your average everyday teenager with a love for design. I specialize in photorealism, icon design, logo design, 3D modeling, and environmental design. My interest in design had started during my freshman year of high school, but hadn't fully sparked until grade 11 when I had taken my first design course, Communication Technology, and with the help of my Communication Technology Teacher, Mr. Di Cesare, he helped me improve my skills. Now I am hosting most of my work on DeviantArt under my alias, 098v, and I have just finished my grade 12 Communication Technology class which has helped me improve my skills to reach new and greater heights.
